THE VISION

The Civilization Stack

2.1 The Regenerative Protocol

This protocol details the philosophical and social architecture of our regenerative civilization. It is built upon the principles of a "Braided Economy," which intertwines multiple forms of capital—natural, social, and financial—to create a resilient and equitable system. A core component is the "Community-Hour (C-hour)," a time-based currency that values every individual's contribution equally, fostering collaboration and community cohesion.

2.2 The Grand Narrative

Our journey is framed by a cosmic story that reinterprets ancient myths as the lost history of a technologically advanced civilization. This narrative explores the solar micro nova cycle, a recurring celestial event that has shaped human history and consciousness. By understanding these cycles, we can unlock the wisdom of our ancestors and prepare for the future.

2.3 The Social OS

The Social Operating System is a set of protocols designed to foster a harmonious and value-aligned society. Key features include:

  • The Guardian of Values Protocol: A decentralized system for upholding our shared principles, as defined in the "Aura Covenant."
  • Global Group Marriages: A framework for fostering deeper connections and collaborative family structures on a global scale.
  • The Aura Covenant: A living document that outlines the core values, rights, and responsibilities of our community members.
