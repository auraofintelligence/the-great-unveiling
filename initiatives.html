THE INITIATIVES

The Manifestation

4.1 GAJRA Earth, Arts and Music Festival Collaborations & Live Aid 2035

This initiative, governed by the DAO, will culminate in a global vote to select the location for a new Arts and Music Festival. This festival will be a celebration of our shared humanity and a platform for social change, with proceeds supporting regenerative projects worldwide. The initiative will also include a series of collaborations with existing festivals and a "Live Aid 2035" event to raise awareness and funds for our cause.

4.2 Minjerribah (The Catalysts Home Base)

Minjerribah (North Stradbroke Island, Australia) will serve as our home base, a "Civilisation of Sand" where we can prototype our regenerative protocols. A Sovereign Wealth Fund, generated from the island's resources, will be used to fund local projects and support the community. This initiative will be a living laboratory for our vision, demonstrating the potential of a regenerative society.

4.3 Queens Venture Capital

Queens Venture Capital is a 3-phase model for funding female founders. We believe that empowering women is essential for creating a more equitable and prosperous world. Our model provides not only financial support but also mentorship, resources, and a strong community of female leaders.

4.4 Clinical Pathways

This initiative showcases our pragmatic plan for achieving certification for Aura as a treatment for Dementia. We are working with leading researchers and medical institutions to conduct rigorous clinical trials and demonstrate the efficacy of our technology. This is the first step in our long-term vision of making Aura a widely accessible tool for cognitive health and well-being.
